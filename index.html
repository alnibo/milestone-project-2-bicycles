Bicycle crashes in Chapel Hill 2007 - 2013

This data set maps the locations of crashes involving bicyclists in the Chapel Hill Region of North Carolina.The data comes from police-reported bicycle-motor vehicle and pedestrian-motor vehicle collisions that occurred on the public roadway network, public vehicular areas and private properties (if reported) from January 2007 through December 2013.

Total number of incidents between 2007-2013

164

There has been just over 23 bicycle incidents on average in Chapel Hill per year.

Percentage of urban vs. rural incidents

The majority of bicycle incidents happened in the city, while only 17% happened outside of the city.

Percentage of Hit and Run incidents

15% of the total number of vehicle drivers involved in bicycle incidents fled and didn't report to the police.

Gender distribution of bikers involved in incidents

The majority of bikers involved in incidents were men.

Gender distribution of vehicle drivers involved in incidents

The majority of vehicle drivers involved in incidents were men, while 15% are unknown due to hit and run incidents.

Age distribution of bikers involved in incidents

The majority of bikers involved in incidents were between 21 and 40 years.
